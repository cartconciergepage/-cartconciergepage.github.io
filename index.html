What is Cart Concierge?

Cart Concierge is a bot dedicated to your online store. It can recommend products for your clients, track delivery status, find the nearest location of your physical store or search by text or photos.

Why do I need a bot?

If your social presence is important and you want to provide meaningful interactions for your customers then you need a bot that is specialized in eCommerce and connected to your store.
